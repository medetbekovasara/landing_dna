06 / FAQ

What the system can and cannot conclude.

Can it read ordinary posts and carousels?

Yes. ContentDNA reads captions and visible image text. Reels and TikTok videos may also include speech transcripts.

What if I do not know my niche or audience?

You can answer diagnostic questions in plain language or leave them blank. The system treats your answers as hypotheses and compares them with the sources.

Does it copy another creator’s style?

No. The profile describes durable rules and observable mechanics. It is designed to preserve identity without reproducing specific content.

What happens when sources contradict my onboarding answers?

The Validator marks the contradiction and may return the profile as needing review instead of pretending the evidence agrees.
